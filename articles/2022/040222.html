Development Blog: Long Time Coming

Pixelate Team April 02, 2022 First article


After years of work, we've finally gotten to a stage where we are comfortable enough to share our progress. Thus, we decided it was time to launch a devlog.

It has been a long process. So far, we've tackled scriptwriting, conceptual designs, re-designing assets, and choosing an engine. Of course, we still have a long road ahead, but every inch of ground we've made has been exciting.

New Version

We've come a long way in terms of defining what we consider to be a distinct style. As you can see in the new style, we went for a more whimsical design to capture more genuine neon noir lighting. More defined lines, jury-rigged technology, and a more detailed 1920s vintage inspiration are all part of the new style.

Old Version

Centropolis is an RPG where you play as an augmented private eye who is in way over his head. When hired by a powerful client, you soon find this case spiraling out of hand.

Solve a case involving kidnapping, industrial espionage, and murder in an electropunk 1920s. We look forward to reading your feedback and ideas!
