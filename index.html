Centropolis

After years of work, we've finally gotten to a stage where we are comfortable enough to share our progress. Thus, we decided it was time to launch a devlog.

It has been a long process. So far, we've tackled scriptwriting, conceptual designs, re-designing assets, and choosing an engine. Of course, we still have a long road ahead, but every inch of ground we've made has been exciting.

Centropolis is an RPG where you play as an augmented private eye who is in way over his head. When hired by a powerful client, you soon find this case spiraling out of hand. Solve a case involving kidnapping, industrial espionage, and murder in an electropunk 1920s.

We look forward to reading your feedback and ideas!
